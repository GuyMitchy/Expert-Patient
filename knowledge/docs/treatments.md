# Ulcerative Colitis Treatments

## Medication Categories

### 5-ASA Drugs
- Mesalamine
- Sulfasalazine
- Balsalazide
- Olsalazine

### Corticosteroids
- Prednisone
- Budesonide
- Hydrocortisone

### Immunomodulators
- Azathioprine
- Mercaptopurine
- Cyclosporine
- Methotrexate

### Biologics
- Infliximab
- Adalimumab
- Golimumab
- Vedolizumab
- Ustekinumab

## Surgery
- Proctocolectomy with ileostomy
- Proctocolectomy with ileal pouch-anal anastomosis (IPAA)

Source: Crohn's & Colitis Foundation, 2023
