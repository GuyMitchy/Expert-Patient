# Lifestyle Management for UC

## Diet Considerations
- Keep a food diary
- Eat smaller meals
- Stay hydrated
- Consider limiting dairy
- Avoid trigger foods
- Low-residue diet during flares

## Stress Management
- Regular exercise
- Meditation
- Yoga
- Deep breathing exercises
- Adequate sleep
- Support groups

## Nutrition
- Iron-rich foods
- Calcium supplements
- Vitamin D
- Folic acid
- B12 monitoring
- Probiotics consideration

Source: NIDDK, 2023
